Apple Reavels New Info About A5 Devices, Following Epic Games lawsuit.

April  9, 2021 13:06 EDT
Share Tweet Submit

And About The Epic Games Claims About Using Our Power In The Market For Killing Off Older Devices With A6 and Older Possessors, The Mid-2019 App Store A5 Dropping Was A maintenance Period That Lasted 5 Months And We Informed Our Customers Via iTunes and Finder That a Restore Is Required To Gain The Apple Services Back, As We Have To Always Optimize Our App Store For Hardware Limitations, And We Didn't At All Intend To Force Our Customers To Upgrade Their Devices
